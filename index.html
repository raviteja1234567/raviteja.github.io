My Hobbies

Inline Skating

Learning a new skill is always challenging, and this was certainly the case when I decided to learn inline skating during my Masters program in the Czech Republic. Despite the difficulty of the task, I was motivated to learn and determined to master this exciting new sport.

Initially, the learning process was difficult, and it would have taken me much longer to learn if I had not had good body balance. After just one week of practice, I was able to skate smoothly on smooth surfaces, but I quickly realized that the real challenge lay in skating on roads and other uneven surfaces. It was important to use safety gear, especially during the early stages of learning, as falling is a natural part of the learning process. Without the proper safety gear, the risk of injury would have been much higher, and I may have even sustained a serious injury.

Despite the challenges and risks, I was fortunate to have my friend Siddhi by my side throughout the learning process.
